01 · The Challenge

Great at the work.
Terrible at finding it.

SkillPrax had the skills, the track record, and a clear offer. But generating a consistent pipeline of high-intent, decision-ready prospects was a full-time job layered on top of client delivery.

Manual prospecting meant scrolling Meta Ad Library for hours, researching companies with no signal on whether they actually needed help — then sending generic outreach into the void.

The result? Zero qualified meetings. Zero predictable pipeline. Revenue entirely dependent on referrals and luck.

What they needed was a system that surfaced brands at the exact moment they were in pain — and wrote the outreach to match, automatically.

✕
3–4 hours of manual research daily
Scrolling the Meta Ad Library by hand. No scoring. No prioritisation. Pure guesswork on who to contact.
✕
No signal = no urgency in outreach
Reaching out to brands with no idea if they're bleeding spend. Generic emails get ignored in under 2 seconds.
✕
0 qualified meetings per week
Weeks of effort. Zero booked calls with decision-ready prospects. Revenue relied entirely on word-of-mouth.
✕
No pipeline visibility
No way to predict next month's revenue. Growth stalled. The prospecting bottleneck was the biggest constraint.

02 · The Solution

AdRadar deployed for SkillPrax.

An autonomous 7-step intelligence pipeline running at 6 AM every morning — delivering pre-scored, pre-enriched, pre-written prospect reports directly into SkillPrax's inbox before the first meeting of the day.

01
Scrape
Scans Meta Ad Library daily for UK eCommerce brands actively running ads. Captures ad count, creative age, and spend signals in real time — then identifies underperforming campaigns using 5 live signals.
Meta Ad Library API
02
Filter
Removes brands running under 30 days (not yet fatigued) and eliminates enterprise accounts outside SkillPrax's target range. Only qualified signals survive.
Smart Filter Engine
03
Enrich
Finds founder name, verified email, LinkedIn profile, employee count, and funding status. No cold outreach to gatekeepers — straight to the decision maker.
Apollo.io
04
Traffic Analysis
Pulls paid traffic dependency % and monthly visit volume. Brands at 60%+ paid dependency are desperate — ads stop and revenue collapses. Maximum urgency.
Similarweb
05
Score (5 Signals)
Runs every brand against 5 live signals: Creative Fatigue, Desperation Testing, Copy Quality, Paid Dependency, Team Size. Outputs HOT / WARM / COLD tier instantly.
AI Scoring Engine
06
Write
Generates 3 hyper-personalised emails per HOT lead — each referencing the brand's exact ad weakness. Not templates. Not generic. Specific, verifiable, converting.
Gemini AI
07
Export + Launch
Pushes everything to Google Sheets + Airtable. Loads HOT leads into Instantly.ai campaign. Fires Slack notification with daily summary before 7 AM.
Instantly.ai · Slack · Sheets

Scoring Signals

S1
Creative Fatigue
60+ days same ad = CPMs rising, CTR falling, frequency burnout. HOT signal.
S2
Desperation Testing
10+ simultaneous ads = no clear winner, account chaos, wasted spend at scale.
S3
Copy Quality
Generic copy = poor conversion regardless of budget. No urgency, no proof.
S4
Paid Dependency
60%+ paid traffic share = brand dies if ads stop. Maximum urgency to fix ROAS.
S5
Team Size Risk
Under 50 staff = no dedicated in-house media buyer. Founder is the decision maker.
